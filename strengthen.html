Strengthening Teenage Youth in Christ

Strengthen your Teenage Child (for children 11–18)

Join your child with other youth, focused on Christ:

  • Monthly Service Projects
  • Weekly Activities focused on Intellect, Social, Physical and Spiritual

Admission Requirements:

  1. Mother is a participating Sister in Zion.
  2. Mother participates in Emma's Relief Society.
  3. Child is tobacco, alcohol and drug free for at least six months.
  4. Mother and Child commit to read the Book of Mormon together.
