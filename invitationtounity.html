Two Churches — Both on the Path

Mosiah 21:31–35, 22:13–14, 25:14–18

Both groups in Mosiah were accepted of God and prepared for something greater through covenant faithfulness.

Likewise today, sincere disciples are walking toward Jesus Christ while preparing for greater unity.

Unity is not the end— It is a historic beginning.
