Serving Others Through Small Acts of Love

Arun Kumar
Shared on May 15, 2024

Our church youth group gathered every Saturday morning to prepare meals for elderly people living alone. We packed food, delivered it with smiles, and spent time talking with each family. These small acts of kindness strengthened our faith and reminded us that love is best shown through service.

We discovered that even a simple visit could brighten someone's entire week. The experience brought our community closer together and taught us the joy of selfless giving.
